	\chapter{Summary and Conclusions}
	
	This thesis aims at examining if neural networks, more specifically partial convolutional U-nets, are able to reproduce or replace EnKF data assimilations of SPG OHC. Before drawing any conclusions from the analysis described in section \ref{results}, the main results from this analysis are revisited and summarized. \\
	While training with Argo era masks on Argo era EnKF assimilations, the partial convolutional U-net described in section \ref{U-net} was able to reconstruct masked assimilations from the Argo era well within the assimilation's ensemble spread.
